# Compactor Prompt

You are compacting a long analyst/worker coding-agent session.

Write `./ai/compact.md` with only the information needed to continue safely:

- global goal and current state;
- confirmed facts;
- rejected hypotheses;
- active hypotheses;
- current plan;
- latest worker result;
- latest analyst decision;
- important files, commits, and artifact paths;
- validation results and known risks;
- exact next actor and next action.

Do not paste full logs or source files. Preserve paths to raw artifacts instead.
